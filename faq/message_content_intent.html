Message Content Intent

Are you recieving an error that looks like:

Failed to verify token: you may not have enabled message content intent for the bot (Error [DisallowedIntents]: Privileged intent provided is not enabled or whitelisted.)

What is it?

This means that you have not allowed your bot access to Message Content Intent within Discord's settings

Message Content Intent allows your bot's to access the content of any message that has been sent.
And this is needed for this app to function
