How to create a Discord Bot

This will teach you how to create a Discord application, not a bot within this app

Steps

  1. Navigate to the Discord Developer Portal, login if you have to
  2. Click "New Application"
  3. Give it a name and accept the terms
  4. On the side pannel, click "Bot"
  5. Click "Add Bot", then "Yes, do it!"
  6. Scroll down and find "MESSAGE CONTENT INTENT", make 100% sure that this is checked or this app won't work at all

Next, you'll want to invite the bot to your server

  1. On the sidebar, click "OAuth2"
  2. Click "URL Generator"
  3. In the scopes area look for "bot", check it
  4. Scroll down and select any appropiate bot permissions, or just select "administrator"
  5. Scroll down again and copy the link at the bottom, open this and follow the steps to add it to your server
